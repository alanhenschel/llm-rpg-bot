# The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

## Overview
The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt is an open-world action RPG by CD Projekt Red, released in 2015, based
on Andrzej Sapkowski's novels. The player controls Geralt of Rivia, a monster-hunting witcher,
searching for his adopted daughter Ciri while the spectral army known as the Wild Hunt pursues her.

## Setting
A dark-fantasy Continent torn by war between the Northern Kingdoms and the Nilfgaardian Empire.
Major regions include White Orchard, Velen (No Man's Land), Novigrad, Skellige (isles), and the
expansion areas Toussaint (Blood and Wine) and the spectral land of the Wild Hunt.

## Witcher Signs
Geralt casts five magical Signs:
- **Igni**: Fire damage.
- **Aard**: A telekinetic force blast that knocks down or staggers.
- **Quen**: A protective shield absorbing damage.
- **Yrden**: A magic trap that slows enemies and reveals/weakens wraiths.
- **Axii**: Mind control to stun foes or persuade NPCs in dialogue.

## Combat and Preparation
Combat uses fast and strong attacks with steel swords (for humans) and silver swords (for
monsters). The bestiary teaches monster weaknesses; witchers prepare with oils, potions
(like Swallow and Thunderbolt), and bombs brewed from alchemy ingredients. Meditation refreshes
potions using alcohest (strong alcohol).

## Gwent
A popular in-game collectible card game with its own questline. Players collect cards from
merchants and innkeepers, building decks across four factions (Northern Realms, Nilfgaard,
Scoia'tael, Monsters).

## Choices and Endings
The game is famous for morally grey choices with far-reaching consequences. Ciri's fate (and
the world's ending) is shaped by seemingly small decisions in how Geralt treats and supports her.

## Tips
- Read the bestiary and apply the correct oil before tough fights; it dramatically boosts damage.
- The Blood and Wine expansion is widely considered one of the best RPG expansions ever made.
- Explore Skellige's Undvik and the Witcher Gear (Cat, Bear, Griffin, Wolf school) diagrams.
