# Dark Souls

## Overview
Dark Souls is an action RPG by FromSoftware, released in 2011 and directed by Hidetaka
Miyazaki. It is renowned for its punishing difficulty, intricate interconnected world design,
cryptic environmental storytelling, and deliberate, stamina-based combat. The player is the
Chosen Undead, fated to determine the future of the cursed land of Lordran.

## Setting
Lordran is a decaying kingdom in the Age of Fire, slowly fading as the First Flame dies. The
undead are branded with the Darksign and risk going Hollow (losing their mind). The world is a
single seamless map centered on Firelink Shrine, connecting Undead Burg, Anor Londo, Blighttown,
Sen's Fortress, and the Kiln of the First Flame.

## Core Mechanics
- **Souls**: The universal currency, used to level up and buy items. Dropped on death; the
  player gets one chance to recover them, losing everything if they die again before reaching them.
- **Bonfires**: Checkpoints that restore Estus Flask healing charges but respawn all enemies.
- **Stamina**: Governs attacks, dodges (rolls), and blocking. Managing stamina is the heart of combat.
- **Poise**: Resistance to being staggered, important for heavy-armor builds.

## Covenants
Optional factions that affect multiplayer and progression, including the Way of White,
Darkwraiths, Sunlight Warriors (jolly cooperation, praise the sun), and the Blade of the
Darkmoon. Many shape the invasion and co-op PvP systems.

## Bosses
Iconic bosses include the Taurus Demon, the Bell Gargoyles, Ornstein and Smough (a brutal duo
in Anor Londo), Sif the Great Grey Wolf, and the final boss Gwyn, Lord of Cinder. Defeating
bosses grants large soul rewards and often key items.

## Tips
- Learn enemy attack patterns and dodge through them rather than tanking hits.
- The Drake Sword (cut the tail of the Hellkite Dragon) is a strong early weapon crutch.
- Manage your Humanity: being human allows kindling bonfires and summoning co-op phantoms but
  also opens you to invasions. "Praise the Sun" and place messages to help other players.
