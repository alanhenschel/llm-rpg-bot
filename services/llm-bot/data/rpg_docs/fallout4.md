# Fallout 4

## Overview
Fallout 4 is a post-apocalyptic action RPG by Bethesda Game Studios, released in 2015. The
player character is the Sole Survivor, who emerges from Vault 111 after being cryogenically
frozen, searching for their kidnapped son Shaun in the ruins of Boston (the Commonwealth).

## Setting
Set in 2287, 210 years after the Great War nuclear apocalypse of 2077. The Commonwealth is the
irradiated remnants of Massachusetts, home to raiders, super mutants, feral ghouls, and the
mysterious synth-producing organization known as the Institute.

## S.P.E.C.I.A.L. System
Character stats are governed by seven attributes: Strength, Perception, Endurance, Charisma,
Intelligence, Agility, and Luck. Each point unlocks perks in a redesigned perk grid that
merges skills and perks into a single progression system.

## Settlements and Crafting
Fallout 4 introduced base building. Players scrap junk for components (steel, wood, circuitry)
and build settlements, assign settlers, set up defenses, supply lines, and resources. The
workshop system underpins much of the late-game economy.

## Factions
- **Minutemen**: Rebuild the Commonwealth, led by the player as General.
- **Brotherhood of Steel**: Tech-focused militants arriving aboard the airship Prydwen.
- **The Railroad**: A secret group working to free synths from the Institute.
- **The Institute**: Advanced underground scientists creating synthetic humans (synths).
The main quest forces a choice that pits these factions against one another.

## Power Armor
Unlike earlier games, power armor is a wearable vehicle requiring fusion cores for power. The
first set (a T-45) is found early atop a Concord building, used to fight a Deathclaw.

## Tips
- Invest in Intelligence early to gain more XP and unlock the Gun Nut and Science perks.
- Hoard junk: aluminum, copper, and adhesive (vegetable starch) are crafting bottlenecks.
- The Charisma-based "Local Leader" perk unlocks supply lines connecting all settlements.
