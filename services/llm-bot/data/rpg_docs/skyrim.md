# The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim

## Overview
Skyrim is an open-world action RPG developed by Bethesda Game Studios, released in 2011. The
player is the Dragonborn (Dovahkiin), a mortal born with the soul of a dragon, prophesied to
stop Alduin the World-Eater from destroying the world.

## Setting
The game takes place in the northern province of Skyrim on the continent of Tamriel, 200 years
after the events of Oblivion. Skyrim is the homeland of the Nords and is embroiled in a civil
war between the Imperial Legion and the rebel Stormcloaks.

## Dragon Shouts (Thu'um)
Dragonborn can learn Dragon Shouts, powerful abilities composed of three words of power in the
dragon language. Notable shouts:
- **Unrelenting Force** (FUS RO DAH): Sends targets flying with a wave of force.
- **Fire Breath** (YOL TOOR SHUL): Exhales a torrent of flame.
- **Dragonrend** (JOOR ZAH FRUL): Forces a dragon to land — essential for the main quest.
Words of power are absorbed from Word Walls scattered across dungeons and unlocked by
absorbing dragon souls.

## Skills and Leveling
Skyrim uses a classless system with 18 skills across three categories: Combat, Magic, and
Stealth. Skills increase through use. Leveling up grants a perk point and lets the player
choose to increase Health, Magicka, or Stamina. Standing stones (Warrior, Mage, Thief) grant
bonuses to skill categories.

## Factions and Guilds
- **The Companions**: Whiterun warrior guild; questline involves becoming a werewolf.
- **College of Winterhold**: The mage guild, focused on arcane study.
- **Thieves Guild**: Based in Riften's Ratway, focused on stealth and theft.
- **Dark Brotherhood**: A guild of assassins worshipping the Night Mother and Sithis.

## Tips
- Smithing, Enchanting, and Alchemy form a powerful crafting loop to create overpowered gear.
- Sneak archery with a high-damage bow is one of the strongest early builds.
- Save often before opening difficult doors or fighting dragons in the open world.
- Use the Aetherium Forge (Dawnguard) and Daedric artifacts for unique end-game equipment.
