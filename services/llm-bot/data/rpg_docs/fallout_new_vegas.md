# Fallout: New Vegas

## Overview
Fallout: New Vegas is a post-apocalyptic action RPG developed by Obsidian Entertainment (many
former Fallout 1/2 developers) and published by Bethesda in 2010. The player is the Courier,
who is shot in the head while delivering a mysterious Platinum Chip and left for dead, then
revived to pursue revenge and decide the fate of the Mojave.

## Setting
Set in 2281 in the Mojave Wasteland around post-apocalyptic Las Vegas (New Vegas), preserved
from nuclear destruction by Mr. House. The strategic Hoover Dam is the center of conflict.

## Major Factions
- **New California Republic (NCR)**: A sprawling democratic federation seeking to annex the Mojave.
- **Caesar's Legion**: A brutal slaver army modeled on ancient Rome, led by Caesar (Edward Sallow).
- **Mr. House**: The pre-war technocrat ruler of New Vegas, controlling the Strip via securitrons.
- **Yes Man / Independent**: The Courier can seize the Mojave for themselves using the Yes Man AI.
The branching ending is decided at the Second Battle of Hoover Dam based on faction allegiance.

## Reputation System
New Vegas tracks reputation separately with each faction and settlement (Liked, Accepted,
Vilified, etc.), plus a Karma system. Wearing faction armor can disguise the Courier.

## Hardcore Mode
An optional mode where stimpaks heal over time, ammo has weight, dehydration/hunger/sleep
deprivation must be managed, and crippled limbs require a doctor or Doctor's Bag to heal.

## Tips
- The early route to New Vegas via the north is dangerous (Deathclaws at Quarry Junction);
  the intended path loops south through Primm, Nipton, and Novac.
- Skill checks in dialogue (Speech, Barter, science skills) open major non-combat solutions.
- The four DLCs (Dead Money, Honest Hearts, Old World Blues, Lonesome Road) tie into the
  Courier's backstory and culminate at the Divide.
